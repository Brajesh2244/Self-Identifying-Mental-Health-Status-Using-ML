Self Identifying Mental Health Status

Psychological instability is a major public health problem, affecting millions of people worldwide. Machine learning (ML) techniques have the potential to revolutionize the diagnosis and treatment of psychological instability by providing fast, accurate, and affordable screening tools. This project presents a novel ML-based approach for detecting psychological instability using a stacked model ensemble of three base models: SVM, random forest, and decision tree with xgboost as a meta model.

TThe proposed approach is integrated with a web interface developed using HTML/CSS and Flask. The user interface allows users to provide their inputs and receive a probability score for psychological instability. If the probability score is greater than 0.5, the user is asked to answer additional questions to assess the severity of their symptoms and classify the type of psychological instability (depression, anxiety, bipolarity, or PTSD).
